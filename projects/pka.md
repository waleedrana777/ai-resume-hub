After every significant action, overwrite the relevant sections above:

```
Last Session Snapshot → update date, last action, who was working
Active Task           → what is currently in flight
Next Action           → the immediate next step when session resumes

In-Progress Files     → files currently being edited or created
```

**When to update:** After delegating a task, after receiving a deliverable, after completing a task, after any owner decision.
**How:** Overwrite this file. Do not append. Keep it current, not historical.
